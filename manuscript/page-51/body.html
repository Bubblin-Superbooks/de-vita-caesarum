Xx.

Having entered upon his office , he introduced a new regulation, that the daily acts both of the senate and people should be committed to writing, and published . He also revived an old custom, that an officer should precede him, and his lictors follow him, on the alternate months when the fasces were not carried before him. Upon preferring a bill to the people for the division of some public lands, he was opposed by his colleague, whom he violently drove out of the forum.
