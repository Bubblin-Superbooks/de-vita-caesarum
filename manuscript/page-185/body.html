Xx. He Conducted In Person Only Two Foreign Wars; The Dalmatian, Whilst He Was Yet But A Youth; And, After Antony’s Final Defeat, The Cantabrian. He Was Wounded In The Former Of These Wars; In One Battle He Received A Contusion In The Right Knee From A Stone—and In Another, He Was Much Hurt In One Leg And Both Arms, By The Fall Of A Fridge . His Other Wars He Carried On By His Lieutenants; But Occasionally Visited The Army, In Some Of The Wars Of Pannonia And Germany, Or Remained At No Great Distance, Proceeding From Rome As Far As Ravenna, Milan, Or Aquileia.
