Soon Afterwards, At Vienne , As He Was Upon The Tribunal Administering Justice, A Cock Perched Upon His Shoulder, And Afterwards Upon His Head. The Issue Corresponded To These Omens; For He Was Not Able To Keep The Empire Which Had Been Secured For Him By His Lieutenants.
