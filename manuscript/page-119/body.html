Lxxxix. Scarcely Any Of Those Who Were Accessary To His Murder, Survived Him More Than Three Years, Or Died A Natural Death . They Were All Condemned By The Senate: Some Were Taken Off By One Accident, Some By Another. Part Of Them Perished At Sea, Others Fell In Battle; And Some Slew Themselves With The Same Poniard With Which They Had Stabbed Caesar .
