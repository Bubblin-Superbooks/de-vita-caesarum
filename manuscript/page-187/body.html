Xxii. The Temple Of Janus Quirinus, Which Had Been Shut Twice Only, From The Era Of The Building Of The City To His Own Time, He Closed Thrice In A Much Shorter Period, Having Established Universal Peace Both By Sea And Land. He Twice Entered The City With The Honours Of An Ovation , Namely, After The War Of Philippi, And Again After That Of Sicily. He Had Also Three Curule Triumphs For His Several Victories In Dalmatia, At Actium, And Alexandria; Each Of Which Lasted Three Days.
