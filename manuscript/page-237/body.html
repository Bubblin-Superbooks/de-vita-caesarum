Lviii. The Whole Body Of The People, Upon A Sudden Impulse, And With Unanimous Consent, Offered Him The Title Of Father Of His Country. It Was Announced To Him First At Antium, By A Deputation From The People, And Upon His Declining The Honour, They Repeated Their Offer On His Return To Rome, In A Full Theatre, When They Were Crowned With Laurel. The Senate Soon Afterwards Adopted The Proposal, Not In The Way Of Acclamation Or Decree, But By Commissioning M. Messala, In An Unanimous Vote, To Compliment Him With It In The Following Terms: “With Hearty Wishes For The Happiness And Prosperity Of Yourself And Your Family, Caesar Augustus, Gods, But The Continuance Of This Your Affection For Me To The Last Moments Of My Life?”
