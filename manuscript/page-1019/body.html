Scribonius Aphrodisius, The Slave And Disciple Of Orbilius, Who Was Afterwards Redeemed And Presented With His Freedom By Scribonia , The Daughter Of Libo Who Had Been The Wife Of Augustus, Taught In The Time Of Verrius; Whose Books On Orthography He Also Revised, Not Without Some Severe Remarks On His Pursuits And Conduct.
