Amongst the rest, he sung “Canace in Labour,” “Orestes the Murderer of his Mother,” “Oedipus Blinded,” and “Hercules Mad.” In the last tragedy, it is said that a young sentinel, posted at the entrance of the stage, seeing him in a prison dress and bound with fetters, as the fable of the play required, ran to his assistance.
