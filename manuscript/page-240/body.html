V.

Augustus was born in the consulship of Marcus Tullius Cicero and Caius Antonius , upon the ninth of the calends of October [the 23rd September], a little before sunrise, in the quarter of the Palatine Hill , and the street called The Ox-Heads , where now stands a chapel dedicated to him, and built a little after his death.
