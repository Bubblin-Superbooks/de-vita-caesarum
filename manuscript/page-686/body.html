But He Was A Man Of Great Arrogance, Prodigality, And Cruelty. When He Was Aedile, He Obliged Lucius Plancus, The Censor, To Give Him The Way; And In His Praetorship, And Consulship, He Made Roman Knights And Married Women Act On The Stage. He Gave Hunts Of Wild Beasts, Both In The Circus And In All The Wards Of The City; As Also A Show Of Gladiators; But With Such Barbarity, That Augustus, After Privately Reprimanding Him, To No Purpose, Was Obliged To Restrain Him By A Public Edict.
