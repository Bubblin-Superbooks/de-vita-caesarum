Xlv. His Death Was Kept Secret Until Everything Was Settled Relative To His Successor. Accordingly, Vows Were Made For His Recovery, And Comedians Were Called To Amuse Him, As It Was Pretended, By His Own Desire. He Died Upon The Third Of The Ides Of October [13Th October], In The Consulship Of Asinius Marcellus And Acilius Aviola, In The Sixty-fourth Year Of His Age, And The Fourteenth Of His Reign . His Funeral Was Celebrated With The Customary Imperial Pomp, And He Was Ranked Amongst The Gods. This Honour Was Taken From Him By Nero, But Restored By Vespasian.
