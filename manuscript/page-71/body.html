Xlix. The Only Stain Upon His Chastity Was His Having Cohabited With Nicomedes; And That Indeed Stuck To Him All The Days Of His Life, And Exposed Him To Much Bitter Raillery. I Will Not Dwell Upon Those Well-known Verses Of Calvus Licinius:

Whate’er Bithynia and her lord possess’d, Her lord who Caesar in his lust caress’d.
