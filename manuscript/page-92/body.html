The spectacles he exhibited to the people were of various kinds; namely, a combat of gladiators , and stage-plays in the several wards of the city, and in different languages; likewise Circensian games , wrestlers, and the representation of a sea-fight. In the conflict of gladiators presented in the Forum, Furius Leptinus, a man of praetorian family, entered the lists as a combatant, as did also Quintus Calpenus, formerly a senator, and a pleader of causes.
