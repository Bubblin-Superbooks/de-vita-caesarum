The day of election, however, being already fixed by proclamation, he could not legally be admitted a candidate, unless he entered the city as a private person . On this emergency he solicited a suspension of the laws in his favour; but such an indulgence being strongly opposed, he found himself under the necessity of abandoning all thoughts of a triumph, lest he should be disappointed of the consulship.
